About

Hi my name is Vince Hodges and I am a director on the board of Mount Lehman Credit Union. I started Board Central to address the pain we were having by using a spreadsheet to manage our risk registry.

Board Central is always upto date. We can easily see which items need to be reviewed and any changes are done during our board meetings. The registry is so much more readable and useful as we manage our risks.

It has reduced the load on senior management, freeing them to actually spend their time managing the organizaton. It's made reviewing and updating the risk registry faster and smoother and a more engaging process.
