A risk registry is an essential part of any enterprise risk management strategy

What is a risk registry? It's a living document that lists the risks that your organinzation might face, their likelyhood of occuring and the impact that they may have. Each one contains a plan for mitigating or handling the risk as well as commentary on the current status or assesment.
